Bisleri - Technical Analysis

🏢 Company Overview

Bisleri is one of India's most trusted packaged drinking water brands. It uses advanced purification technologies to ensure safe and consistent drinking water across the country.

💧 Source & Treatment Process

  • Source: Groundwater / Municipal supply
  • Reverse Osmosis (RO)
  • UV purification
  • Ozonation for disinfection
  • Mineral re-addition for taste balance

📊 Water Quality Table

Parameter Value Ideal Range
TDS 100 mg/L 50–150 mg/L
pH 7.0 6.5–8.5
Hardness 80 mg/L 60–120 mg/L
Calcium 30 mg/L 20–75 mg/L
Magnesium 10 mg/L 10–30 mg/L
Sodium 5 mg/L <50 mg/L

🧪 Minerals Present

  • Calcium – supports bone health
  • Magnesium – helps muscle and nerve function
  • Potassium – supports hydration balance
  • Controlled Sodium – safe for daily intake

❌ Minerals Absent / Reduced

  • Silica (removed during RO process)
  • Natural trace minerals
  • Bicarbonates (low)

✅ Advantages of Drinking Bisleri

  • Consistent and reliable quality
  • Balanced mineral composition
  • Safe for daily consumption
  • Easily available across India
  • Good taste due to controlled TDS

⚠️ Disadvantages

  • Not natural mineral water
  • Artificial mineral addition after purification
  • Loss of natural trace elements
  • Plastic bottle environmental impact

Aquafina - Technical Analysis

🏢 Company Overview

Aquafina is a globally recognized packaged drinking water brand known for its high purity levels. It undergoes intensive purification, making it one of the cleanest forms of drinking water.

💧 Source & Treatment Process

  • Source: Municipal water supply
  • Reverse Osmosis (RO)
  • Activated carbon filtration
  • UV treatment
  • Micro-filtration

📊 Water Quality Table

Parameter Value Ideal Range
TDS 20 mg/L 50–150 mg/L
pH 7.0 6.5–8.5
Hardness Very Low 60–120 mg/L
Calcium Very Low 20–75 mg/L
Magnesium Very Low 10–30 mg/L
Sodium Low <50 mg/L

🧪 Minerals Present

  • Minimal minerals (trace levels only)
  • Very low sodium

❌ Minerals Absent / Reduced

  • Calcium (almost removed)
  • Magnesium (very low)
  • Natural mineral content
  • Electrolytes

✅ Advantages of Drinking Aquafina

  • Extremely pure water
  • Very safe (low contamination risk)
  • Light and clean taste
  • Suitable for short-term hydration

⚠️ Disadvantages

  • Very low mineral content (not ideal for long-term use)
  • Does not provide essential electrolytes
  • Flat taste for some users
  • Over-purification removes beneficial elements
